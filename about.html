Our Story

Bakes by Noémie launched in April 2020 when pastry chef Noémie decided to pursue her dream of running her own business. Having trained in a prestigious pastry school in Paris and worked with the best chefs in New York, London and Tokyo, it was finally time for Canadian-born Noémie to settle down in South Wales and open her very own bakery. Since opening, Bakes by Noémie has received tremendous praise and made several appearances in magazines and on television. The team at Bakes by Noémie is always expanding.
